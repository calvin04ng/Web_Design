Our partners and collaborators

Australian Science Innovations

Australian Science Innovations delivers challenging science extension programs for high school students that aim to broaden horizons, identify capabilities and build Australia’s scientific community.

AAMT

The leading organisation that supports and promotes mathematics education in schools in Australia, including professional learning, collaborative research and projects, advocacy, events and journals.

AMSI

The collaborative enterprise of Australia’s mathematical sciences. It exists to give independence to these disciplines and provide infrastructure so that they can take initiatives on the national and international stage.

Aust MS

The national society of the mathematics profession in Australia. Its mission is the promotion and extension of mathematical knowledge and its applications. It represents all professional mathematicians in Australia, both pure and applied.

MAV

An innovative and leading association that promotes the importance of mathematics to society through its programs and services. It has more than 1,400 members from all sectors of education including individuals, schools, universities and other institutions.

MAWA

A not-for-profit organisation whose goal is to improve mathematics education across Western Australian. MAWA is a vibrant organisation that provides support for anyone with an interest in mathematics including teachers, parents and students.

ATSIMA

The Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Mathematics Alliance (ATSIM)A is an Indigenous-led charity, and the only national organisation specifically committed to creating ways of teaching and learning mathematics that connect to Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander histories and cultures.

University of Canberra

UC is Australia’s fastest rising university, and one of the fastest rising in the world. It’s also #1 in ACT for full-time employment and starting salaries for three years running.

AAS

To advance Australia as a nation that embraces scientific knowledge and whose people enjoy the benefits of science.

Optiver

As a tech-driven trading firm, Optiver improves financial markets by providing liquidity to exchanges across the globe. At Optiver, we are passionate about investing in the next generation of STEM talent.

Australian Government

Curious Minds is funded by the Australian Government Department of Education through the Women’s Economic Security Package 2018.

UNSW

The University of New South Wales is a strategic higher education partner of the AMT, with a focus on expanding and improving the informatics (computer science) high performance pathway.
